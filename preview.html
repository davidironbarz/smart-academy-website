About Smart Academy

Founded in 2013 with a vision to provide quality education in Monrovia, Smart Academy has been nurturing young minds and building character for over a decade, serving 376 students with excellence.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character in every student, serving 376 students with excellence since 2013.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Monrovia, producing well-rounded future leaders.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Respect, and Service to community guide everything we do.

Our Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive educational programs designed to meet the developmental needs of each age group.

Nursery School

Ages 2-4. Early childhood development through play-based learning.

  • Sensory play activities
  • Basic motor skills development
  • Social interaction and sharing
  • Early literacy introduction

Kindergarten

Ages 4-6. Foundation building for academic success.

  • Pre-reading and pre-writing skills
  • Basic mathematics concepts
  • Science exploration
  • Creative arts and crafts

Primary School

Ages 6-12. Comprehensive academic program.

  • Core subjects (Math, English, Science)
  • Social studies and history
  • Physical education
  • Computer literacy
